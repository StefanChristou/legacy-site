Mobile Research web app

This app was produced to provide information for students on conducting research using smartphones. It is designed to go anywhere, on any web-capable device, including laptops, tablets and mobiles.

The app includes a map of interesting places around London that could be useful for primary research

I have also added a Harvard Referencing section that will allow students to find out how to format references for a range of both primary and secondary sources.

Use the app by navigating to rmr.ravewebmedia.co.uk.
